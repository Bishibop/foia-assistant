HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY - VACATION TIME

Policy Number: HR-2024-03
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

ANNUAL LEAVE ACCRUAL:
- 0-5 years service: 13 days annually
- 6-10 years service: 20 days annually
- 11+ years service: 26 days annually

USAGE GUIDELINES:
- Minimum 4-hour increments
- Maximum 30 days carryover
- Supervisor approval required
- 2-week advance notice preferred

BLACKOUT PERIODS:
- End of fiscal year (Sept 15-30)
- Budget preparation period (Feb 1-15)

Contact HR for questions or exceptions.
