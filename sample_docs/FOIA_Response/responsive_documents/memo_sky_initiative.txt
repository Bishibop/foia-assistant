INTERNAL MEMORANDUM

To: Department Heads
From: Director Thompson
Date: January 10, 2024
Re: New Sky Initiative

This memo outlines our new sky initiative for aerial monitoring systems. This initiative is separate from ongoing projects and will focus on:

- Drone technology assessment
- Satellite imagery procurement
- Weather pattern analysis

Please note this is NOT related to the software development project. This is purely focused on environmental monitoring capabilities.

Budget allocation: $500,000
Timeline: 6 months
